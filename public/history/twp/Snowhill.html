Historical Township Boundaries

SNOWHILL TOWNSHIP
Date
Event
ca. March 1829 Created from parts of Beaver Twp. and Cussewago Twp. 1
Boundaries:  Beginning at the N. W. corner of [Summerhill Twp.], being the S. W. corner of a tract returned to A. Power, and running east by line of said tract to the S. E. corner of a tract in the name of Philip Scott; thence north to the N. E. corner of a tract in the name of H. Carlin; thence west along the line of Erie county to a post, being the N. E. corner of [Beaver Twp.]; thence south to the place of beginning.
12 Nov. 1829 Name changed to Spring Twp. [q.v.]

1 Crawford Co. Court of Quarter Sessions.  The court papers have been lost, and the information presented here is from a condensed report of the commission appointed to determine the new boundaries, printed in the Crawford Messenger of 26 Feb. 1829.   RETURN
