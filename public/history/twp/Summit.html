7 March 1843 Created from parts of Sadsbury Twp. and Summerhill Twp. to replace Harmansburg Twp. 1
Three ranges of lots or tracts of land are hereby set off from the south side of Summerhill township, and three ranges of tracts on the north side of Sadsbury township, in the county of Crawford, are hereby declared a new township, to be called Summit, in lieu of said township of Harmansburg, which is hereby repealed ....
