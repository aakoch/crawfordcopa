Name:  Blooming Valley Cemetery
Location:
Along the west side of Twp. Rt. 676 near where it intersects U.S. Rt. 198, about one-half mile northwest of the intersection of U.S. Rts. 77 & 198 at Blooming Valley borough, and at the east end of Woodcock Dam lake.
Size:  Over 1500 burials, dating from at least the 1840s.
Condition:  Good; still in use.

Indexes:
1. Mr. & Mrs. Beryl Braymer, [untitled] (ts., ca. 1998), 69 pp., compiled from an index at the Historical Society and from cemetery association records.

Burial records:
