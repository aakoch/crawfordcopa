     REMARKS: Water in this Township has nearly all a brackish mineral taste. Soil a stiff clay loam well adapted for grazing. Face of the whole township an unbroken level. Timber White & Black Ash, Hickory Birch, & Sugar Maple. The unusual frost of June 4th & Sept. 16th destroyed most of the spring & fall crops in the township as well as in the whole county.
