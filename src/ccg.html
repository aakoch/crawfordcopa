Crawford County, Pennsylvania

What is CCG?

To subscribe

Crawford County Genealogy is the title of both the journal and the newsletter issued by the Crawford County Genealogical Society.  Publication began in the spring of 1978, with Thomas L. Yoset as Editor, and is now in its thirty-second volume.

The journal was formerly published twice a year, in February and August.  Each issue contained about 65 to 85 pages of compiled family histories and source materials, plus an every name index.   CCG is the sole journal at the county level to be included in a list of the most scholarly genealogical periodicals. 1

For information on the contents of past journals, see the Topical Index.   A table of contents and every name index to the latest journal are available on-line; it is hoped that these will be expanded to include back issues.  CCG is also indexed in Annual Genealogical Periodical Index and in Periodical Source Index (PERSI).  Certain back issues are available for purchase, and many genealogical libraries carry CCG.

In 1997, the Society began publishing a May newsletter featuring members' ancestry tables.  The November newsletter was basically a dues notification, but also includes lists of Society acquisitions and new members.
