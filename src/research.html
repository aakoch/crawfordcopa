RESEARCH SITES

Crawford County Historical Society library
Located on the first floor of the Helene Barco-Duratz Cultural Center at 411 Chestnut Street, Meaedville, PA&nsbp;&nsbp;16334; free parking in rear
Is open to the public for a $5 charge (free to members) on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, and Friday afternoons from 1 to 5, Thursday from 1 to 9, and on Saturday from 9 to 3.
Also houses the collection of the Crawford County Genealogical Society
Copies of most published sources on the history of Crawford County are available in closed stacks
Photocopies must be made by staff, and cost 25 cents/page (letter size).
The most notable feature is an extensive card catalogue indexing notices of marriages, deaths, and births in Meadville and (for certain years) Linesville and Conneautville newspapers.  Most of the indexed papers have been microfilmed and are available in the Fiction Room.  (The Titusville Herald is available on microfilm from 1865 to about 1890, but has not been indexed.)
