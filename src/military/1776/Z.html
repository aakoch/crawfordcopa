REVOLUTIONARY WAR VETERANS

MARTINUS ZEILIE 
Sources
  1. Pension Abstracts p. 4006: ZIELIE, Martinus or Martinus Zelie, NY Line, S28960, sol was b at Middleburgh in Schoharie Co NY (no date given) & he lived there during the Rev War & he lived there until 1815 he moved to Friendship in Allegany Co NY to live with his son Peter Zielie & after 9 yrs he moved with this son to Oil Creek in Crawford Co PA where sol d 2 Nov 1833, sol's wife (not named) had d in 1813 at Aurelius NY, sol's son Peter Zielie appl 16 appl 16 Aug 1844 at Mentz in Cayuga Co NY aged 58, other children shown were; David Zelie of Oil City PA, Cornelia wife of John Rose of Savannh in Wayne Co NY, Cornelius Zelie of Elmir in Chemung Co NY, Ann Zielie who lived Mentz NY with her bro Peter Zielie, Agnes wife of Isaac Ward of Painted Post in Steuben Co NY & Thomas Zielie of Lysander in Onondaga Co NY
  2. Note:  DAR Patriot Index, 3333: ZEILEY: ZEILE, ZIELLEY Peter: b 10-16-1733 NY d 12-9-1806 NY m Cornelia Becker LCol NY
