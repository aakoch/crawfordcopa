Research Facilities
Crawford County Historical Society library - check website for times when open (Wednesday through Saturday 9 to 3 starting September 2022)
Note:  The Historical Society library has reopened at its new facility in the Tarr Mansion (townhouse) on Diamond Park, next to the Crawford County Judicial Center; use the side entrance along Walnut Street.  Handicapped (only) parking is available in the rear.  Two hour metered parking is available around Diamond Park and on North Main Street (free on Saturdays); some non-metered parking is available along Walnut and Liberty Streets.
Admission fee:  $5 to non-members, free to members; membership fees start at $20 annually
        The Historial Society library also houses the collection of the former Crawford County Genealogical Society.
        A surname index to genealogies fills two drawers of the card catalogue.
        Photocopies [except from microfilm] must be made by staff, and cost 25 cents/page (letter size).
For holdings, see “Resources for CCHS” prepared by CCHS staff.
Note:  The most notable feature of the Historical Society’s collection is an extensive card catalog indexing notices of marriages, deaths, and births (to 1950) in Meadville and (for certain years) Linesville and Conneautville newspapers.  Most of the indexed papers have been microfilmed and are available on three readers in the library; photocopies (available on the newest machine only) cost 25 cents per page (pay at the desk).  (The Titusville Herald is available on microfilm from 1865 to about 1890, but has not been indexed.)

Crawford County Judicial Center, Meadville
Location:  on Diamond Park (between North and South Main Streets), beside the Crawford County Courthouse
Hours:  Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
            closed holidays
Parking:  around Diamond Park (25 cents/hour); some free parking on side streets
            alleys on both sides the Courthouse (two hour limit)
For holdings, see Courthouse
Office of the Clerk of Courts (including Orphans’ Court) is to located on the ground (second) floor, to the right of the main entrance.
Prothonotary’s Office is located on the ground (second) floor, to the left of Sheriff’s Office.

A public snack area, with vending machines and microwave oven, is located on the first floor.
