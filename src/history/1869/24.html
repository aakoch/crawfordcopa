CHURCH DIRECTORY.
 
    Baptist—Corner of Centre and Second sts.  Services at 10½ A. M. and 7 P. M. ; Sabbath School 12 M. ; Lecture and Prayer Meeting Wednesday evening.  Rev. R. H. Austin, pastor.
    Christ Church (Episcopal)—Corner of Walnut street and Public Square.  Services at 10½ A. M., and 7 P. M. ;  Sunday School at 2 P. M. ; Prayer Meeting Wednesday evening.  No regular pastor.
    Lutheran (German)—Corner of Pine street and Chancery lane.  Services at 10½ A. M..  No regular pastor.
    Methodist Episcopal—Corner of Chestnut and South Main sts.  Services at 10½ A. M. and 7 P. M. ; Sunday School at 9 A. M. ; Prayer Meeting on Thursday evening.  Rev. John Peat, pastor.
    Methodist Episcopal (African)—Corner of Liberty and Arch sts.  Services at 10½ A. M. and 7 P. M. ; Suuday School 3 P. M. ; Prayer Meeting Wednesday evening.  Rev. W. H. Brown, pastor.
    First Presbyterian (O. S.)—Corner of Liberty and Centre sts.  Services at 10½ A. M. and 7 P  M. ; Suuday School 9 A. M. ; Prayer Meeting Wednesday evening.  Rev. J. G. Carnachan, pastor.
    Second Presbyterian (N. S.)Centre st.  Services at 10½ A. M. and 7 P. M. ; Sunday School 3 P. M. ; Prayer Meeting Wednesday evening.  Rev. R. Craighead, pastor.
    St. Bride’s (Roman Catholic)—Arch st.  Rev J. L. Finucane, pastor.
    St. Agatha’s (Roman Catholic, German)—Corner of Liberty and Pine sts.  Rev. M. J. Decker, pastor.
    St. Paul’s (German Ref.)—Park Avenue.  Rev. L. D. Leberman, pastor.
    Unitarian—Corner Chestnut and South Main sts.  No regular pastor.
    Independent German Ref.—Corner of Poplar and South Main sts.  Rev. Robert Kœhler, pastor.
 
SOCIETIES AND LODGES.
 
    Crawford Lodge, No. 234, A. Y. M.—Meets first and third Thursday of each month ; hall Water street.  A. Fleisher, W. M.
    Lodge 408, A. Y. M.—Meets 1st Monday of each month.  J. H. Dick, W. M.
    Solomon H. R. A. Chapter, No. 191—Meets first Tuesday of each month, at Masonic Hall.  F. H. Foster, H. P.
    North Western Commandery N`o. 25.—Meets fourth Tuesday of each month at Masonic Hall.  S. B. Dick, E. C.
    Cussewago Lodge, No. 108, I. O. O. F.—Meets at Odd Fellows Hall every Monday evening.  A. McMichael, N. G.
    Olympus Encampment No. 82, I. O. O. F.—Meets at Odd Fellows’ Hall 2d and 4th Thursdays of each month.  C. P. Baker, C. P.
    Harugari Lodge.—Meets every Monday evening.  Hall opposite Gable House.
    King Solomon Temple of H and T. 36.—Meets every Tuesday evening at Betts’ Block, Water street.
    Germania Moenner-Chor.—Meets at Blystone’s Hall, Chestnut street, on Thursdays and Fridays of each week.
    Post Reynolds No 98, G. A. R.—Meets Friday  evenings at Lyceum Hall.
    Knights of Pythias.—Austin Lodge, No. 164, meets every Thursday evening.  A. B. McCarty, W. C.
