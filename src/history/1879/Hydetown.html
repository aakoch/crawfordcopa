OIL CREEK BORO,
MORE commonly known as Hydetown, from the Hydes who were among the first settlers, and largely owners of the territory within its limits, was organized as a borough in 1869.  It is situated in the western part of the township, on Oil Creek, about three miles from Titusville, on the line of the Pittsburgh, Buffalo & Titusville, and the Union & Titusville Railroads.
