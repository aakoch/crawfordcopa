EVANSBURG.
 

     This beautiful village, situate on the banks of Conneaut Lake, seven miles from Meadville, is one of the oldest towns in Crawford County.
     It is the principal town on Conneaut Lake.  It contains three churches, two hotels, and about three hundred inhabitants.  Its business houses compare favorably with any town in the county.  The Lake House, on the opposite of the Lake, is now one of the finest hotels in the county.  Messrs. Fairman & Hall, its proprietors, take great pains in the accommodation of guests, and as Conneaut Lake affords the best of sport for the angler and sportsman, we anticipate a lively time during the coming season for Evansburg.
