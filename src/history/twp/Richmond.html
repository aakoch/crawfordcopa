Historical Township Boundaries

RICHMOND TOWNSHIP
Date
Event
ca. March 1829 Created from parts of Randolph Twp. and Rockdale Twp. 1
Boundaries:  Beginning at the N. E. corner of [Woodcock Twp.]; thence southwardly along the line of said town ship to the S. W. corner of donation lot No. 1502; thence eastwardly by the line of tracts to the S. E. corner of lot No. 1389; thence northwardly by the lines dividing tracts to the N. E. corner of lot No. 1398, being the north side of the seventh donation district; thence west along said line to the S. E. corner of a tract in the name of E. Felton; thence north by said tract to the S. E. corner of lot No. 150, in the eighth district; thence north by line of said tract to the N. E. corner of the same; thence west by the line of tracts to the N. W. corner of lot No. 160; thence south to the N. E. corner of a tract returned to H. Eck; thence west by the north line of said tract to the place of beginning.
1 Crawford Co. Court of Quarter Sessions.  The court papers have been lost, and the information presented here is from a condensed report of the commission appointed to determine the new boundaries, printed in the Crawford Messenger of 26 Feb. 1829.   RETURN
