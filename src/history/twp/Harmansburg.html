HARMANSBURG TOWNSHIP
Date
Event
11 July 1842 Created from portions of Sadsbury Twp. and Summerhill Twp. 1
The Harmansburg school district, in the county of Crawford, is hereby declared a separate township election district, for township purposes....
7 March 1843 Dissolved upon formation of Summit Twp. [q.v.].2
“That the eighty-second section of an act of assembly, approved the eleventh day of July, one thousand eight hundred and forty-two, entitled ‘An Act regulating election districts, and for other purpose,’ which creates Harmansburg school district into a separate election district, be, and the same is hereby repealed ....”
