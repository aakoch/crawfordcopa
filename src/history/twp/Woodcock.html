Historical Township Boundaries

WOODCOCK TOWNSHIP
Date
Event
ca. March 1829 Created from parts of Mead Twp. and Rockdale Twp. 1
Boundaries:  Beginning at the S. W. corner of a tract late of the heirs of David Mead; thence eastwardly to the S. E. corner of a tract in the name of Jas. Doughty; thence north to the N. W. corner of a tract in the name of Thomas Frew; thence east to the N. E. corner of the same; thence south to the N. W. corner of a tract in the name of Jas. Herriott; thence east to the western line of the seventh donation district; thence along said line to the S. W. corner of tract No. 1515; thence east to the S. E. corner of tract No. 1517; thence northwardly to the north line of said district; thence west to the S. E. corner of a tract in the name of Roger Eck; thence north to the N. E. corner of a tract in the name of Geo. Eck; thence west to the N. W. corner of a tract in the name of Paul Eck; thence north to the N. E. corner of a tract in the name of F. Rasburg; thence west by the north line of said tract & the north line of A. Humes' to the western margin of French Creek; thence down the same to a point west of and opposite the place of beginning; thence across the Creek to the place of beginning.
27 April 1844 Portion incorporated as the borough of Woodcock [q.v.]
10 April 1849 Portion incorporated as the borough of Saegerstown [q.v.]
12 Nov. 1858 Southwestern part lost to Mead Twp. [q.v.]
17 May 1867 Portion incorporated as the borough of Blooming Valley [q.v.]
2 March 1870 Part lost by annexation of County farm to borough of Saegertown [q.v.]

1 Crawford Co. Court of Quarter Sessions.  The court papers have been lost, and the information presented here is from a condensed report of the commission appointed to determine the new boundaries, printed in the Crawford Messenger of 26 Feb. 1829.   RETURN
