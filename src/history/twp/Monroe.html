MONROE TOWNSHIP
Date
Event
1808 Proposed, but not created; the Court adjusted the northern boundary line and instead named the township Wayne [q.v.] 1
Proposed boundaries:  Beginning at French Creek at the upper Corner of the Tract of land now occupied by Fredrick Baum and from thence an East course until it strikes little Sugar Creek thence up the water Course of said Creek to the line of the Seventh Donation District thence Eastwardly along the said line to the eastern Extremety of said Township thence southerly &c until it meets the place of beginning and lands lying south of the first described line.
