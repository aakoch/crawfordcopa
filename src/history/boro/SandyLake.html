9 July 1800 Created from parts of Neshannock Twp. and Irwin Twp., Mercer Co.1
Boundaries:  Beginning at the North East corner of Salem Township, thence Eastwardly with the County line to the corner thence Southwardly until the line strikes the south line of the ... fourth District of Donation Land thence with the same Westwardly to the corner of Salem Township thence to the [place of] beginning.
July 1801 Divided into Sandy Creek Twp. [q.v.] and Cool Spring Twp.2
