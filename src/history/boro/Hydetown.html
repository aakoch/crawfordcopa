OIL CREEK / HYDETOWN
Date
Event
Origins: Settled by James Kerr, James Curry, and Peter and Daniel Titus in 1797.  Oil Creek P.O. established 1848, but “more commonly known as Hydetown, from the Hydes, who were among the first settlers, and largely owners of the territory within its limits.”1
