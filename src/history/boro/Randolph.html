13 Aug. 1823 Created from the eastern part of Mead Twp. 1
Boundaries:  Beginning at the South West Corner of Donation Lot in the 7th District No 1493 Thence running Northerly dividing Two ranges of Donation tracts to the North West corner of Donation lot in the 7th District No 1511 Thence Easterly along the lines of Rockdale & Bloomfield to the north east corner of Donation Lot in the 7th District No thirteen hundred and twenty three - Thence southerly along the line of Oil-creek Township to the line of Wayne Township at the South East Corner of Donation Lot in the 7th District No thirteen hundred & forty one (No 1341) and thence Westerly along the South line of the Seventh Donation District to the place of Beginning.
ca. March 1829 Boundaries redefined: eastern and northern parts lost to creation of Athens Twp., Richmond Twp., and Troy Twp.; territory gained from Wayne Twp. 2
