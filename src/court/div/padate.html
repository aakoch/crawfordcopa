SUPREME COURT DIVORCES
The following are divorces granted by the Pennsylvania Supreme Court, as printed in the Pennsylvania Genealogical Magazine 1(1898):185-92, but listed here chronologically, covering the years 1785 through 1801 (together with five from 1801 through March 1804 added from other sources).  In April 1804, county common pleas courts were empowered to grant divorces,1 and after 1805, the Supreme Court was prohibited from hearing divorce cases.2

Divorces were also granted by the Pennsylvania Legislature until 1874; see Statutory Divorces or the introduction thereto.
