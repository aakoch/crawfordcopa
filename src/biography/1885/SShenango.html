      J. W. WEST, farmer, P. O. Jamestown, Mercer Co., Penn., was born in this townshiop, October 9, 1855, and is a son of Asa and Mary (Ralston) West, native of New York State.  His grandparents on his father's side were Robert and Mary (Warner) West, who settled in Mercer County in 1833, and there lived until 1845, when they finally settled in Ashtabula County, where he died in 1860, his wife following in 1868.  They had ten children:  Rosina, Ervin, Asa, Hannah, Susan, Marian, Sarah A., Annetta, Angenett, and one who died in infancy.  Asa West, the father of our subject, was married, December 5, 1839, to Mary, daughter of John and Hannah Ralston, by whom he had two children:  Laurinda M., who died in infancy, and J. W.  Our subject was married, October 15, 1878, to Amanda, daughter of J. C. and Mary J. McAdoo.  They have one childï¿½Viola R.  Mr. West occupies the old home farm, and looks after the interests of his aged parents, which are, of course, identical with his own.  He is a bright, intelligent young man.
