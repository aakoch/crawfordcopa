Crawford County, Pennsylvania


Cemeteries
Note:  Transcriptions of tombstones from most of the cemeteries in the county are available at the library of the Crawford County Historical Society in blue three-ring binders, arranged by township.  These vary in quality (some including merely the year of birth and death), and few are verbatim transcriptions.  All of the late Eugene F. Throop’s typed cemetery compilations were given to your host in 2015, and some of his material is being added to this site.
